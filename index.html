FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register on TWITHA?
Click "Join Free" on the homepage, fill in your name, email, phone and password. After registration, complete your KYC to unlock all features. It takes less than 5 minutes.
What documents are needed for KYC verification?
You'll need a valid government-issued ID: National ID (NIN Slip), Driver's License, International Passport, or Voter's Card. You'll also need your NIN, BVN, and a selfie holding your ID. Pay the ₦500 convenience fee via the WhatsApp link provided.
How do I view my transaction history?
Login to your account → navigate to the "Activity" tab at the bottom of your dashboard → all transactions appear with full details. You can also filter by type and download a PDF statement.
Can I get a loan with TWITHA?
Yes! Verified members with consistent contribution history can apply for loans up to ₦150,000. Loan approval is based on your savings record. Apply directly from your dashboard's Activity section.
How long does KYC verification take?
KYC documents are reviewed within 24 hours on business days. You'll receive a notification once your verification is complete.
How do I withdraw my savings?
Go to the Wallet tab → enter amount (minimum ₦5,000) → select your linked bank account → submit. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours. Ensure your bank account is verified under Profile Settings.
