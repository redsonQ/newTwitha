What's the document required for KYC?
Twitha Contribution Plus account verification requires a valid ID card (National ID / NIN Slip, Driver's License, International Passport, or Voter's Card).
How to view the transaction history?
1. Login to your account via the homepage
2. You will be redirected to your dashboard
3. Tap the Activity tab at the bottom navigation
4. All your transactions appear here with full details
Can I get a loan with no interest?
Yes! But you must first be a registered member of Twitha Contribution Plus and have a consistent contribution history to qualify.
How much time does the verification process take?
It takes just a few minutes to submit. Admin review is typically completed within 24 hours.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when registering, including your full name, phone number, email, BVN, valid ID, and bank details for KYC compliance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide and improve our thrift and contribution services, process transactions, assess loan eligibility, send important notifications, prevent fraud, and comply with Nigerian financial regulations.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information with trusted partners such as banks, payment processors, and regulatory authorities when required by law. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We use bank-grade encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits to protect your information.
